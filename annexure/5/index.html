Annexure 05  ·  Shelf-life

Barrier properties & shelf-life.

The hard part of replacing laminate isn’t breakdown — it is keeping a moisture-sensitive, aromatic product fresh for nine months.

Pan masala is a brutal product to package: hygroscopic, volatile, and demanding a 9–12 month shelf life in Indian ambient. MittiSeal matches the moisture and aroma performance of a 3-layer PET/Al/PE laminate while remaining fully compostable.

5.2 Accelerated shelf-life study

Product filled in MittiSeal pouches and stored at 40°C / 75% RH for 90 days (equivalent to ~12 months ambient, per ASAP). Parameters tracked weekly:

  • Moisture uptake: +1.8% over 12 months equivalent (acceptance threshold: <3%).
  • Aroma intensity by sensory panel: 4.2 / 5 at month 12 vs 4.5 / 5 at month 0.
  • Microbial count: within FSSAI limits throughout.
  • No delamination, no seal failure, no pinholing under vibration testing (ASTM D999).

5.3 Known limitations

Oxygen permeability is higher than laminate. For product lines where oxidation is the primary failure mode (not the current pan masala brief), a secondary inner layer of PLA-coated Kraft is available at a ~8% cost premium. This option is not required for the reference product.
