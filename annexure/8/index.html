Annexure 08  ·  Compliance

Regulatory & food-contact safety.

The regulations MittiSeal must pass to be sold, and the test evidence that it does.

MittiSeal is designed to clear Indian FSSAI packaging regulations and BIS food-contact standards without exemption, and to qualify as compostable under CPCB’s Plastic Waste Management Rules — so that the brand buying it carries no regulatory risk.

8.3 Declarations required on pack

  • Compostable symbol with IS 17088 certification number.
  • Home-compost & soil-compost icons with disposal text in English + regional language.
  • EPR registration number of the brand owner.
  • Seed-species declaration and “plant after use” guidance.
  • FSSAI logo and batch / manufacturing information.

8.4 Certifications targeted

  • CPCB compostable plastic certification, valid for two years.
  • BIS licence for IS 17088 marking.
  • TUV Austria OK Compost HOME — international recognition.
  • DIN CERTCO Seedling mark for industrial compostability.
  • FSSAI material compliance certificate under Schedule III.
Regulatory posture is conservative by design. MittiSeal never asks the regulator or the brand to make an exception — only to apply existing rules to a sachet that already passes them.
